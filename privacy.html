Privacy Policy

Harborlight — Last updated April 16, 2026

Harborlight is developed and maintained by Michael Poulter, based in Florida, United States. If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact michael@vastocielo.com

The short version

Harborlight does not collect, store, transmit, or share your personal data. Everything stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, no databases, and no way to see anything about you.

What stays on your device

The following information is stored locally on your iPhone only:

  • Your first name, used to personalize your experience
  • Your selected techniques and preferences
  • Your journal entries and session history, if you choose to enable journaling
  • Your Health integration preferences

None of this information ever leaves your device. It is not uploaded, transmitted, or accessible to anyone including us.

Apple HealthKit

If you choose to connect Harborlight to Apple Health, we write the following data to your personal Health app:

  • Mindfulness session duration after each technique session
  • State of Mind entries based on your post-session check-in responses

We never read any data from Apple Health except as described below. Your health data remains under your full control within the Apple Health app.

Heart Rate (optional)

If you choose to enable heart rate tracking and wear an Apple Watch:

  • Harborlight reads your most recent heart rate from Apple Health immediately before and after technique sessions only
  • This data is displayed to you within the app to show how the session affected your body
  • Heart rate data is never uploaded, transmitted, or stored outside of your device
  • You can disable this feature at any time in Settings
  • This feature is entirely opt-in and Harborlight functions fully without it

Resources content

The Resources section of Harborlight fetches a list of articles, videos, and crisis support links from a publicly hosted file. This request contains no personal information and no user data of any kind.

Third party services

Harborlight does not use any third party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services. We do not display ads. We do not sell data. We do not share data. There is no data to share.

Children

Harborlight does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no account creation, no data collection, and no transmission of personal information.

Your rights

Since all data lives on your device, you are always in complete control. You can delete all app data at any time by resetting Harborlight in Settings, or by deleting the app entirely.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how Harborlight handles data, we will update this policy and note the date of the change at the top.
